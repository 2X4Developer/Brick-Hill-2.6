Player Controls:
Space - Jump
WASD - Movement
P - Restart Game (/change team)
I - Zoom in
O - Zoom out (soon making scroll wheel)

Build Game:
B - Change brush (paint color)
N - Change brick (size)
R - Rotate brick
T - Tilt brick
Numpad Arrows - Move brick horizontally
Up/Down Arrows - Move brick vertically
Enter - Place brick
Click - Move brick relative to view
Tab - Ingame help

Cops vs Robbers:
Click - Use tool
